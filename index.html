Hello! I'm Andy, a second year Computer Science / Computer Engineering student at the University of Southern California. Currently, I work as an intern with Dr. John Heidemann at the ANT Lab in Los Angeles. My interests lie in the fields of network security and data analysis.

Research

I'm working on the Retrofuture Project, where I conduct research on data anonymization techniques and create / fiddle with packet capture and data visualization tools. This summer, I'm helping with a paper on data anonymization in data-sharing projects for individual and organizational privacy at the Los Alamos National Laboratories. Recent work includes:

  • Probe Plotter, a data visualization tool for tracking /24 block outages in ISI data. Completed for the Trinocular Project.
